How to Use:

  1. Important: Must be served via HTTP, not file://
    Run python -m http.server 8080 then open http://localhost:8080
  2. Wait for both models to load (status shows "Ready")
  3. Click "Start Recording" and select a browser tab with Teams/Zoom
  4. Enable "Share tab audio" in the dialog
  5. Allow microphone access when prompted
  6. Both meeting audio and microphone will be captured
  7. Analysis panels will update automatically
  8. Click "Export Markdown" to download your meeting notes
